# Dou-BA Evaluation Video Design

## Style Prompt

Use a dark cyber basketball broadcast style that matches the live demo UI: neon cyan borders, red/blue team contrast, amber highlight notes, glassy cards, and fast but readable review pacing. The video should feel like a serious product demo for platform evaluation, not an exaggerated advertisement.

## Colors

- `#06101f`: deep navy court background.
- `#0ea5e9`: cyan UI frame and tech glow.
- `#ff5a6b`: red team and hit feedback.
- `#4eb5ff`: blue team accent.
- `#facc15`: review highlight and key information.
- `#f8fafc`: primary text.

## Typography

- Primary: `"Microsoft YaHei", "PingFang SC", sans-serif`.
- Use heavy weights for section titles and compact readable weights for explanatory captions.

## Motion

- Slow push-in on real app screenshots to keep the video feeling recorded from the current build.
- Use short neon tags to call out review requirements: setup, team join, like shot, gift shot, settlement.
- Keep overlays in safe margins and do not cover the hoop or settlement panel.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use cartoon-only visuals that differ from the current implementation.
- Do not overstate randomness, betting, reward, or cash-out language.
- Do not cover the actual UI with oversized captions.
- Do not introduce new colors unrelated to the app.
