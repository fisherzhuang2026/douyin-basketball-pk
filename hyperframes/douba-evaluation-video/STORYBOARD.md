# Storyboard

## Beat 1: Opening

Duration: 0-5s. Show the current app shell and introduce Dou-BA as a live interactive basketball shooting PK game.

## Beat 2: Host Setup

Duration: 5-11s. Focus on host configuration: match duration, team names, keywords, and the fixed like rule note: 30% hit rate, +1 on hit.

## Beat 3: Team Join

Duration: 11-16s. Show red and blue viewers already joined, with team cards and online counts visible.

## Beat 4: Like Shot

Duration: 16-22s. Show like-triggered shot and real-time scoreboard/leaderboard updates.

## Beat 5: Gift Shots

Duration: 22-34s. Show low and mid gift states, then high gift impact with stronger particles and scoring.

## Beat 6: Settlement

Duration: 34-44s. Show final settlement panel with winner, scores, MVP, and team contribution lists.

## Beat 7: Compliance Close

Duration: 44-50s. Summarize that the proposal is team PK interaction only and avoids lottery, betting, cash reward, or rebate language.
